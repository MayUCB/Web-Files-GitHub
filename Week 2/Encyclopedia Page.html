Xiao Zhan

---Xiao Zhan is a Chinese actor and singer. He started his acting career in 2016 and had since won widespread attention for his dramas, including Douluo Continent (2021), The Untamed (2019), and has won a number of awards and nominations for his roles.

Xiao Zhan (Sean Xiao)

  • Born: October 5, 1991 (age 29), Chongqing, China
  • Alma Mater: Modern International Art Design Academy, Chongqing Technology and Business University
  • Occupation: Actor, Singer

As a singer, Xiao Zhan released a single "Made to Love" (Spot Light) at the end of April 2020. With a sales volume of 25.48 million, it set the Guinness World Record for the fastest-selling digital album within 24 hours of release.
