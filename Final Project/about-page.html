Enjoy the life that tea brings
Organic herbal tea shop that delivered hot tea to your doorstep.

T.Life app is a tea delivery app for consumers to select, schedule, and sit back with same-day delivery or pickup.
Dating back to Ancient Egypt and Ancient China, herbal tea has been used for hundreds of years for its health benefits and great taste.
Our Story:

The founders of TLife are two young women from Egypt and China, Amy Hassan and Lisa Zhan. They both love the beautiful mood and culture of the tea-tasting process, then they hit it off in the first meet. Five years later, they opened a take-out herbal tea shop in Boulder City that could deliver hot tea to your house. People can enjoy the life that tea brings.
